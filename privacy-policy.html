Pocket Register – Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 9, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Pocket Register does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users. The app functions entirely on your device, and your data remains under your control.

2. Advertising

Pocket Register uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect certain non-personally identifiable information such as device identifiers, approximate location, and ad interaction data to provide personalized and non-personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

3. In-App Purchases

The app offers an optional in-app purchase to remove ads. Purchases are processed securely through Apple’s App Store. We do not have access to your payment details.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your data to external servers.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this page with a revised effective date.
